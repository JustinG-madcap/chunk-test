Executions

The Executions view provides an overview of all on-going and completed executions along with their status, environment, start time, duration, trigger type, and top severity. Here, you can:

  • Export the list of executions as a .csv file.

  • Select any execution to rerun it, view it in the Detailed view, or open it on the canvas.

To view all executions:

  • Click the menu icon at the top left and select Executions. The Executions view opens.

To export a list of all executions as a .csv file:

  • In the Executions view, click EXPORT.

To perform actions on a selected execution:

  1. In the Executions view, click the required execution. The view changes to show information on the selected execution only.

  2. At the top right, click the required option:

    • View on canvas

    • Detailed view

    • Rerun
