Access the Help Center

Our Help Center is always just a click away! Here is what you can do when you are inside the TestCraft UI:

  • To open the Help Center, click the Help button on the right.

  • To minimize the Help Center, click the X at the top right.

The Information pane for elements in your test flow works just the same: Select an element and click the info iconon the toolbar to display the pane. Click the X at the top right or click the icon again to close the pane.
