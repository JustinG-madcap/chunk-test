Test flow creation

In , the area where you build your test flow is called the canvas. This is your creative area where you design your scenarios.

The canvas is available in Basic Mode for simple, linear tests and actions and Advanced Mode for more complex flows and scenarios and more advanced actions.

To access the canvas:

  • In the SpecsTests view, click a spec.

  • In the Executions view, click an execution. Then click View on canvas at the top right.

    The spectest opens on the canvas, in Advanced Mode.

Following are some of the things you can do on the canvas:

  • Adding a test step

  • Changing an element's name

  • Changing a test step's action

  • Removing a test step

  • Duplicating a test step

  • Toggling a test step in or out of the flow

  • Removing an element's binding

You can perform most actions in both modes, but our procedures walk you through using Basic Mode wherever possible.
