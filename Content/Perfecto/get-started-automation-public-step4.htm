4 | Select a device

  1. On the Perfecto landing page, under Manual Testing, click OPEN DEVICE.

  2. In the Manual Testing view, on the My Devices tab, do the following:

    To open a mobile device
    1. In the left pane, select the All mobile, Android, or iOS folder.
    2. Select the device you want to test on.
      The details pane opens on the right, displaying information on the selected device.
    3. On the CAPABILITIES tab, select your scripting language to view the generated capabilities.
    To open a web device
    1. In the left pane, select the Web folder.
    2. Click OPEN NEW WEB SESSION.
    3. In the Open new web session panel, select the required configuration.
    4. At the bottom right, click View capabilities.
      The right pane opens showing the capabilities for the selected device.
    5. Select your scripting language select your scripting language to view the generated capabilities.
  3. In your script, replace parameters based on the generated capabilities as applicable.
